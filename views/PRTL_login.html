Why we created ReviewVerse

Interested to know about the college you are applying to? Wanna get in touch with the alumini to get a fairer more details? OR maybe It is a friday night and you want to know about the best restaurants in the area you would be travelling to..

We at reviewVerse have been through the same at each stage of our life and that is why we have come up with the this awesome new platform. Decison making is anyways difficult and worse of all if you have no one to ask questions too, ReviewVerse aims at easying you out with your decision helping you to connect to people where you can ask for their opinion.Looking for a coaching institute to help you with your aim of JEE you rush to the many names suggested in the newspaper advertisments and are satisfied at none, why not check out a former student had reviewed about it on reviewVerse, Let's save that energy for your studies, ok?!. We do suggest you to pick out your favourites and then go for a personal visit.

There is nothing worse than going out for dinner and then having to eat substander food, what could be even worse is being charged exorbitant amount for the fish on your plate which was stinky and stale. We at reviewVerse want you to have a great meal so before you step out, do check out the star rating of the restaurant you are visiting and incase you do not find the restaurant we'll be happy to welcome your opinion on the restaurant you just visited! You can add a topic of your choice and categorise it in whichever field it belongs and BAZINGA you have helped a lot people looking for the review of that particular topic!

Let all your worries about visiting the wrong place end, may you find the best of what you are looking for. ReviewVerse gives you the chance to make every decision easier from looking for the best grocery shop in the area near you to the next place you would like to visit on you weekend get away! Just Rate everything to make the community bigger and better.

Every opinion counts folks! so lets get going and start with a journey towards less drama and more value for money. Create a free account and start with rating and writing a review for your previous institution or the most recent cafe you visited. Lets help the community better.
